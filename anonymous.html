Ars Poetica
By Anonymous

Poems are the silent words inside my head.
Persistently nagging at my subconscious,
Unable to find another escape route

They serve as a time capsule
For when I want to remember how the young me
Experienced the world

Poetry is what makes your heart swell with warmth
Even when your teeth are chattering and your lips are purple
Poetry is the pit in the bottom of your stomach as you
Realize the door you closed behind you is now locked and you have no key

Poetry is what makes the smallest details the most monumental,
What feeds the chatter in between my ears
