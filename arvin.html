Ars Poetica
By Arvin Krishnan

May a poem be more than what it seems.
May the words sparkle off the page,
into the reader's imagination.
May it give you an indescribable feeling,
unique to that poem.

Poems are the ripples left in water,
after the skipping of a rock.
Let it reach into your soul.
Then you may be touched.

May a poem find the missing part of you,
and fill the bitter void.
May it widen your smile,
or deepen your horror filled face.
May a poem be whatever you need it to be.
So may you please,
let the poem be a poem.
