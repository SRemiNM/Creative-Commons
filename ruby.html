Falling Asleep
By Ruby Meyers

Eyes, body, mind
In that order it goes
I am a broken down car
My feet deflated like
Wheels
They need the air pump
Or sleep
Please let me
I am trying to fall
Into that endless bliss
Dreaming is not bliss
Dreaming is empty
Like an unfinished story
Like roots of a tree
Always reaching and branching
but never ending
I am a broken down car, and so are you, right?
